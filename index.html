Fatal Police Pursuits Dashboard

This dashboard examines fatal police pursuits across the U.S. by race, location, and cause of pursuit.

In recent years, growing public attention has focused on the often-deadly consequences of police interactions, but one area that remains key is the fatality risk associated with police vehicle pursuits. This project explores these fatal pursuits in terms of racial disparities, geographic distribution, and types of offenses involved, utilizing calendar heatmaps and treemaps to show how risk varies.

Global Terrorism Dashboard

This dashboard explores trends in terrorist activity from 1970–2019 by region, method, and frequency.

This global terrorism dashboard visualizes regional and temporal trends in terrorist activity (1970–2019), showing variation in attack types and regional prevalence using bubble and stacked area charts from the Global Terrorism Database.

Gun Violence Dashboard

Analyzes gun violence patterns by state, weekday, and season between 2014–2017.

Gun violence remains a deeply entrenched issue in the United States, affecting communities in a variety of different ways depending on factors such as when and where incidents occur. This project analyzes these variations and provides a clear look at impact rates across different time frames and geographic areas.

U.S. Crime Trends Dashboard

Tracks national crime rates by type and region from 1960–2022.

Understanding crime trends in the United States requires a comprehensive analysis of both historical data and regional patterns. This project maps those patterns using FBI data, with particular attention to South Carolina and property crime fluctuations over time.

Incarceration Trends Dashboard

Visualizes jail and prison incarceration by county across South Carolina.

The incarceration rate in the U.S. declined from 2019 to the lowest level since 1995. This dashboard investigates local disparities in South Carolina, highlighting how rural-urban divides, local law enforcement policy, and socioeconomic status affect incarceration rates at the county level.

Campus Crime Dashboard

Compares crime stats at major South Carolina universities, normalized per 100k students.

This dashboard focuses on sexual misconduct, aggravated assault, and burglary trends from the five largest universities in South Carolina. It explores normalized statistics, revealing sharp contrasts in campus safety and crime reporting practices.
